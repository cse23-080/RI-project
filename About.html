Who We Are

FarmersAuction is a revolutionary online marketplace that bridges the gap between farmers and consumers. Founded in 2015 by a group of agricultural enthusiasts, our platform empowers farmers to get fair prices for their produce while giving consumers access to fresh, high-quality farm products.

We believe in sustainable farming practices, fair trade, and building strong relationships between producers and consumers. Our transparent auction system ensures that everyone gets the best value possible.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading agricultural marketplace

Founded in 2015

Started as a small local initiative connecting 5 farms with consumers in Fresno, California.

Platform Launched (2016)

Developed our first online auction platform, expanding to 50 farms across California.

Innovation Award (2018)

Recognized by the USDA for our innovative approach to agricultural marketplaces.

National Expansion (2020)

Expanded operations to 45 states with over 500 participating farms nationwide.

Today & Beyond

Continuing to innovate with new features while staying true to our founding principles.
